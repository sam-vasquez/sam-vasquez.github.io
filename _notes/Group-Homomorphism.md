---
A group homomorphism is a function $\phi: G \rightarrow H$ that maps one group $(G,\cdot)$ to another group $(H,*)$ in such a way that the group structure of $G$ is preserved in $H$:
$$
\phi(g_1 \cdot g_2) = \phi(g_1)*\phi(g_2).
$$
