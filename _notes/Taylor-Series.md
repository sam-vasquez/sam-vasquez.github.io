$$
f(x) = f(a) + f'(a) (x-a) + \frac{1}{2}f''(a)(x-a)^2 +\ldots
$$

Examples:
$$
e^{ax} \approx 1 + ax + \ldots
$$
$$
\frac{1}{x} \approx \frac{1}{a} - \frac{1}{a^2}(x-a), a > 0.
$$
$$
\frac{x-a}{x} = 1 - \frac{a}{x} \approx 1 - a \left[\frac{1}{a} - \frac{1}{a^2}(x-a)\right] = \frac{x-a}{a}.
$$
