$$
\sinh x = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{2}, \cosh x = \frac{e^x + e^{-x}}{2}.
$$
$$
\sinh 0 = 0, \cosh 0 = 1, \tanh 0 = 0.
$$

$\sinh x$ and $\tanh x$ are odd. $\cosh x$ is even. 
$$
1 - \tanh^2x = \frac{1}{\textrm{cosh}^2x}.
$$
$$
\tanh x  \approx x - \frac{1}{3} x^3.
$$
